ScamShield was built to fight the growing wave of digital fraud targeting everyday Indians. From fake KYC alerts to lottery scams, millions of people lose their hard-earned money every year.

We are a team of three engineering students from Bengaluru, built this for our country with passion . Our mission is simple — use Google Gemini AI and Firebase to give every Indian a free, instant scam detection tool right in their browser.
